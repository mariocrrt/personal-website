What is Vimium?

Vimium is a free browser extension available for Google Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers. It takes inspiration from the legendary Vim text editor, renowned for its speed and efficiency.

How Does Vimium Work?

At its core, Vimium transforms your browsing experience by enabling you to navigate the web entirely using keyboard shortcuts. With Vimium installed, you can say goodbye to the mouse and perform all your browsing tasks with lightning speed.

Key Features of Vimium

  • Effortless Link Navigation: Jump to links on a page with ease using intuitive keyboard commands.
  • Tab Management Made Simple: Seamlessly switch between tabs, close tabs, and open new tabs without lifting a finger.
  • Smooth Scrolling: Scroll through web pages smoothly and precisely using Vimium's keyboard-based scrolling.
  • Intuitive Page Navigation: Navigate forward and backward through your browsing history effortlessly.
  • Customization Options: Tailor Vimium to your preferences with customizable keyboard shortcuts and settings.
  • Extension Compatibility: Vimium plays well with other browser extensions, ensuring a seamless browsing experience.
